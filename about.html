queer.link is the personal webpage of Gwen Hope, LGBTQ/GSM/MOGII educator and speaker. (In the future, it might contain online resources as well, after she expands the website.)

Gwen Hope has a very broad education, however one of her most extensively-studied areas is LGBTQ/GSM/MOGII issues. This is due to a very personal reason - Gwen herself is a queer, pansexual transgender woman.

Gwen started speaking and educating about these subjects during her tenure as a student, in which she noticed that many of the textbooks used by the professors were incorrect, whether due to outright invention, poor sourcing, or merely being sorely out of date.

Since her advent speaking, she's been called back repeatedly by more and more professors/instructors each semester they teach.
